Frequently Asked Questions

How does OAuth work?

You'll connect your social media accounts securely through OAuth. Just click "Connect LinkedIn" or "Connect Twitter", authorize the app, and you're done. We never see your passwords - it's the secure industry standard.

What happens when I hit my limits?

We use soft limits - you'll see a friendly reminder when you're close. We'll even let you post a few extra times before gently suggesting an upgrade. No hard blocks, no frustration.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your billing dashboard. You'll still have access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption for all data. OAuth tokens are stored securely, and we follow industry best practices for security. Your data is never shared with third parties.

How does the AI caption generator work?

We use Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate captions tailored to your niche and platform. Just enter your topic, select your niche, and get 3 unique variations instantly. Works for LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram.

Can I manage multiple brands/clients?

Yes! Pro plans support 3 accounts, Business plans support 10 accounts. Perfect for agencies, freelancers, or anyone managing multiple brands.
